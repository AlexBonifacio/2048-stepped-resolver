    Getting started

    Copy the board you see in the game, enter the current score, then start.

    Move with the arrow keys, place the spawned tile by left clicking an empty cell, and press Enter to confirm the tile or calculate the AI suggestion.

    Right click to lower a tile. Click Undo if you hit a tile by mistake.

    Hands-free: calibrate the screen capture below, then click Watch game. The board and the suggestion follow the game screen by themselves.
