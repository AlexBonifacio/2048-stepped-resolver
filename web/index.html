    Getting started

    1. In Screen capture below, click Calibrate and drag a rectangle around the game grid. This is needed once, as long as the game window does not move.

    2. Click Watch game. The board follows the game screen by itself: a fresh game starts automatically, and for a game in progress you just confirm the estimated score.

    3. Play the suggested move in the game. The board and the next suggestion update on their own.

    Manual mode (no capture): build the board by clicking cells (right click lowers), enter the score, press Start. Move with the arrow keys, click the cell where the new tile spawned, press Enter to confirm. Undo reverts a mistake.
